About Me

Who I am

Welcome to my digital space. I'm a Mid-Level Software Engineer with a proven track record of architecting and delivering mission-critical SSO and user authentication APIs at an enterprise scale, serving over 80,000+ employees and beyond, across Fortune 500 manufacturing environments.

My expertise spans a code stack consisting of Node.js/TypeScript with Python used in targeted backend services. My cloud and DevOps experience includes extensive hands-on experience with AWS Services (ECS Fargate, EC2, RDS, S3, KMS, Secrets Manager, SSM Parameter Store, CloudFormation, and more) culminating to completing an AWS Cloud Practioner Certification in 2023. My hands-on experience also includes robust DevOps practices and technologies like Terraform for provisioning our AWS infrastructure and taking ownership of all CI/CD Pipelines with TeamCity using Github access keys to ensure secure and scalable deployment solutions.

Currently building skills in AI and machine learning, I'm dedicated to making learning more accessible and personalized to escape "tutorial hell".

My background in research psychology has given me unique insights into how technology can transform education. Driven by a passion for innovative education technology, I'm now actively exploring AI/ML. My personal venture, 'The Pocket Professor,' is an AI-powered learning assistant built on local LLM inference, embodying my vision for accessible and personalized educational tools. I'm uniquely positioned to blend technical excellence with pedagogical insights, creating solutions that truly enhance the learning experience.
