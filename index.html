Customized Learning

The Pocket Professor

This app serves as a mock graduate-level college instructor of sorts, creating customized learning plans to provide users with a personalized syllabus on any subject they wish to learn!

My background in experimental psychology as well as my own subjective experience with self-learning made me deeply skeptical of popular neuromyths like "learning styles." My goal is to combine peer-reviewed seminal research and meta-analysis with natural language processing tools to build an engine based on proven learning mechanics—like Active Recall and Spaced Repetition—to make your learning experience more efficient and most importantly: effective.

I designed the app to appeal to my fellow autodidacts on their self-teaching learning journey, breaking down their learning path with week-by-week lessons based on their skill level on the subject (novice, intermediate, expert), and their time committment.
