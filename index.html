AI Tutor App

The Pocket Professor (coming soon!)

My AI tutor app serves as a mock graduate-level college instructor, creating customized learning plans to provide users with a personalized syllabus on any subject they wish to learn!

Using my background in experimental psychology, I'm combining peer-reviewed seminal research in adaptive learning, combined with natural language processing tools and machine learning concepts to "learn" the users' learning style to maximise their capacity to absorb the material they wish to learn.

I designed the app to appeal to my fellow autodidacts on their self-taught learning journey, breaking down their learning path with week-by-week lessons based on their skill level on the subject (novice, intermediate, expert), and their time committment.
